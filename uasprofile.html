📖 My Diary

Hello, my name is Annisa Wening Galih 💗

I’m a soft-hearted girl with big dreams. I love pink and black, but I also adore soft colors. I cherish peaceful days and find beauty in the quiet, simple things that hold meaning.

I’m a deep thinker, a dreamer, and someone who always tries to stay kind — even when days feel heavy. I truly believe that softness is a form of strength, not weakness ✨

I love journaling, listening to music, watching anime, taking cute photos, making vlogs, and collecting little things that bring me joy. I’m deeply in love with Naruto, pandas, and matcha — everyone close to me knows it well. But of course, there are many other things I love that I can’t even name one by one 🌷

I choose to live slowly and softly — not because life is easy, but because I choose peace. I value honesty, warmth, and safe spaces for the heart 💕

I hope this little corner feels like a warm hug. May you feel calm, understood, and loved — just like the soft world I dream of 🌈
